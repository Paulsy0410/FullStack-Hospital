About Us

When you need answers, you know where to go.

Welcome to City Hospital, where compassionate care and cutting-edge medical expertise converge to create a healthcare experience like no other. Established in 2000, City Hospital has been serving the Thrissur community and beyond with unwavering dedication to your health and well-being.

Our Mission

At City Hospital, our mission is simple but profound: to provide exceptional medical care to our patients, embracing the values of integrity, compassion, and innovation. We are committed to promoting a healthier, happier community by delivering the highest standard of healthcare services.

Our Philosophy

At the heart of our mission lies the belief that exceptional healthcare should be accessible to everyone. We aim to create a warm and supportive healing environment where every patient feels heard, respected, and treated with compassionate care.

Our Vision

To provide healthcare of the highest quality to all in a spirit of compassion and continually improve the standard of care provided to the community through the promotion of value-based quality education and research.
